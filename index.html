About

MujheBachao is a webpage that helps users to contact emergency services with just the push of a button. Once the button corresponding to the desired emergency serveices is clicked, emergency services are notified immediately with the user's locaion for rapid assistance. The user further has the option to provide additional details to help the emergency services team with their response.

No need to dial a number and wait for it to connect. Just click on the service required, and emergency services will automatically be notified and on their way.
